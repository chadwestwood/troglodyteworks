Meeting files are historical records of what was discussed and decided at the time. They are not automatically current architecture contracts.

- `PM0001.md` — initial planning
- `PM0002.md` — first DC001 public deployment; later superseded by Railway
- `PM0003.md` — product discovery and Community direction
- `PM0004.md` — subsequent product planning

When a decision changes, preserve the meeting record and add or update an ADR under `Decisions/`.
