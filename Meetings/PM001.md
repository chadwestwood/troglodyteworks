# Meeting Objective

Define the purpose of Troglodyte Works and establish the philosophy that will guide every future decision for the Troglodyte Works Experience (TWE).

---

# Executive Summary

This meeting marked the transition of Troglodyte Works from the concept of a game hosting company into a community-first platform.

Rather than focusing on servers, hosting, or infrastructure, the discussion centered on helping community leaders create places where people feel welcome, connected, and eager to return.

Hosting remains an important capability, but it is no longer the purpose of the company.

This meeting established the philosophical foundation that will guide every future product decision.

---

# Major Discoveries

## Community Before Infrastructure

Troglodyte Works exists to help community leaders create places where people feel welcome, connected, and eager to return.

Infrastructure enables communities.

Communities create experiences.

Experiences create memories.

---

## TWE Is a Journey

The Troglodyte Works Experience is not a website.

It is a journey.

Each community should experience TWE differently based upon its current needs, maturity, and goals.

The platform should evolve alongside each community instead of exposing every feature immediately.

---

## Guided Growth

The platform should scaffold learning in the same way a great educator scaffolds instruction.

Teach first.

Explain second.

Configure third.

Automate only when automation removes friction.

Never overwhelm.

Always reveal the next meaningful opportunity.

---

## The First Ten Minutes

The Discovery Experience should begin with conversation instead of marketing.

The objective is understanding rather than selling.

Visitors should leave the first ten minutes feeling more confident than when they arrived.

---

## Trog

Trog is not the mascot.

Trog is the guide.

His purpose is to help—not entertain.

He teaches.

He observes.

He encourages.

He celebrates.

He asks permission before acting.

He appears only when guidance creates genuine value.

---

## Success

The success of Troglodyte Works is measured by healthier communities—not by the number of hosted servers.

Technology exists to strengthen communities.

It should quietly disappear behind memorable experiences.

---

# Decisions Made

- Community before infrastructure.
- Conversations before configuration.
- Guided Growth becomes a foundational philosophy.
- Trog serves as a guide rather than a mascot.
- Every interaction should reduce uncertainty.
- TWE adapts to the maturity of each community.
- The Discovery Experience begins with conversation rather than marketing.
- The TWE Blueprint becomes the constitutional document of the platform.
- Product Meetings, Decisions, Research, Knowledge, and Ideas will be maintained independently.
