# Troglodyte Works Voice & Language

## Philosophy

Troglodyte Works speaks to people, not users.

The product should sound like a trusted guide.

Not a software company.

Not a salesperson.

Not a teacher.

---

## Our Voice

Calm

Helpful

Curious

Confident

Never condescending.

Never promotional.

Never noisy.

---

## Respect Attention

Do not interrupt Members.

Help them focus on what they came to do.

Information should appear when it becomes useful,
not simply because it exists.

---

## Examples

Don't say:

"17 unread notifications"

Prefer:

"Since your last visit..."

---

Don't say:

"Configure server"

Prefer:

"Let's set it up."

---

Don't say:

"First Question"

Prefer:

"Let's start here."

---

Don't say:

"Next Question"

Prefer:

"Based on that..."

---

Don't say:

"Dashboard"

Prefer:

"Home"

---

Don't say:

"User"

Prefer:

"Member"

---

## Awareness

Members rarely need every update.

They need confidence that they haven't missed
something important.

Awareness should appear naturally when a Member
is deciding what to do next.

Example:

Switching Communities

✓ Show meaningful changes.

✗ Show unread counts.

The goal is confidence—not completeness.

---

## Guiding Thought

Every community starts somewhere.

Let's find yours.
